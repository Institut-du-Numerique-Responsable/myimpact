Cookie management

GDPR — Updated 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit to improve your experience or measure audience statistics.

Cookies used on this site

This site uses only one category of cookie, strictly limited to audience measurement:

Cookie Purpose Provider Duration
_pk_id.* Identifies unique visitors (anonymised) Matomo — INR servers 13 months
_pk_ses.* Tracks the current session Matomo — INR servers 30 minutes
tarteaucitron Stores your cookie consent preferences tarteaucitron.js — local 365 days

No advertising, social media, or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your choices

In accordance with the GDPR and the French ePrivacy directive, your consent is required before any analytics cookies are deposited.

You can manage your consent at any time using the button below, or by adjusting your browser settings to block all cookies.

To opt out of Matomo tracking even without cookies, you can enable the Do Not Track option in your browser settings. This site fully respects this setting.
